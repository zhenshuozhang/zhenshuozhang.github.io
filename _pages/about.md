I am a graduate student (2021 - 2024, expected) in [Zhejiang University](https://www.zju.edu.cn/english/), supervised by Prof. Siliang Tang. Previously, I received the B.Eng. degree from [CS](http://www.en.cs.zju.edu.cn/), [ZJU](http://www.zju.edu.cn/english) in 2021.

My current research focuses on graph neural networks, especially pre-trained graph neural networks, and on using GNN for scientific discovery. During my undergraduate period, I had some research experience on quantum information at [QICI](https://qici.weebly.com/), HKU, supervised by Prof. Giulio Chiribella and Prof. Ravishankar Ramanathan.

I am deeply passionate about exploring the intersection of graph neural networks and AI for science, fervently committed to harnessing the transformative power of GNN to drive profound advancements across diverse scientific disciplines.
